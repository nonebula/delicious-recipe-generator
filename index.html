Chicken Biryani

Biryani is a mixed rice dish that was thought to have originated from Iran before settling in modern India. It is made with rice, some type of meat and spices. To cater to vegetarians, in some cases, it is prepared by substituting vegetables for the meat. Sometimes eggs and/or potatoes are added.

Chicken Fried Rice

Thai fried rice is a variety of fried rice typical of central Thai cuisine. In Thai, khao means "rice" and phat means "of or relating to being stir-fried." This dish differs from Chinese fried rice in that it is prepared with Thai jasmine rice instead of regular long-grain rice.
